The Best Movies According to Nasir

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Source Code

A soldier brought back from death by science to solve someone else's mass murder. But he doesn't know how long he has to live.

Fight Club

A man trying to cope with his mundane existence accidentally creates a violent cult with his mysterious friend.

An aged Bruce Wayne is forced to once again relearn what it is to be Batman and save Gotham in its worst state yet.
